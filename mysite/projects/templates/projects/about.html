I was medically discharged and came back home to Iowa, from there I went to join the workforce. I started with the electricians trade school Tesdell electric, i stayed on for three more years and dew to lay offs i left. This got me thinking I needed to set an example for my future kids like all of the people who did the same for me. This led to my decision to join the best immersive boot camp around, General Assembly, to start my career as a software engineer.
